Courtney Christine Gainor

Courtney graduated from UC Santa Barbara in June 2018 with a degree in Mathematics and a minor in Math Education. During her time there, she played defense for the UCSB Women's Lacrosse Team, in addition to taking on the role of the team's Treasurer for several years. She also began as a writer, then a question master, and eventually worked her way up to the highest position of coordinator of the university sponsored health education website sexinfoonline.com, an internationally reaching site dedicated to spreading accurate information about the human body, healthy relationships, and safe sexual practices.

Courtney is currently continuing her role as a student through her enrollment in the Data Science and Visualization Bootcamp via UC San Diego's Extension Program. She is studying to pursue a career in the data science field, and appreciates the challenging hands on practice that is encouraged during the course.
